ASSCAT Student Life AI Assistant

Problem Statement
Students often struggle to manage their daily campus life, such as finding events, locating offices, and tracking deadlines. The current system is not centralized and requires too much manual searching.

Industry 5.0 Justification
This AI assistant enhances student experience by providing personalized and real-time assistance. It supports students in making better decisions while still allowing them to control their academic and personal activities.

 Agile User Stories
1. As a student, I want to ask the AI about upcoming school events, so that I can participate and stay engaged.
2. As a student, I want to find campus offices using AI, so that I can quickly locate where to go.
3. As a student, I want reminders for deadlines, so that I can manage my time better.
